# Twenty³

Twenty³ keeps your data on your device. No account, no sync service, and no server-side storage.

<p class="hero-actions">
  <a class="button" href="{{ '/privacy-policy/' | relative_url }}">Read the Privacy Policy</a>

## Privacy by design

- Your data stays local to your device
- Camera access is only used when you choose to capture an image
- Import, export, and reset are handled entirely on-device
